Welcome to Cubus!

Cubus let's you create a custom Ubuntu Server specifying the type of OS you'll be using and provide a list of packages that you can autmatically include on your system.

You can also choose to include Docker images on your system that will be pulled and ready to be used on your server.

You'll be also provided with a tool to monitor your system remotely.

For any additional information read the Guide page.

Recommended minimum system requirements
  • 2 GHz single core processor or more
  • 2 GB RAM (system memory) or more
  • At least 10 GB of hard drive
  • A USB port for the installer media (on physical hardware)
  • Internet access is required
  • If possible, dedicating an extra CPU core, and an extra GB of RAM will greatly increase the responsiveness of your system
