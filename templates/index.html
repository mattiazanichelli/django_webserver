Welcome!

Here you can customize your Ubuntu Server ISO choosing which type of OS you'll be using and provide a list of packages that you want to be included in your system.

You can also choose to install Docker on your system and include some images that will be pulled and ready to be used on your server.

Recommended minimum system requirements:
Physical hardware
  • 2 GHz dual core processor
  • 4 GiB RAM (system memory)
  • 25 GB of hard drive space
  • VGA capable of 1024×768 screen resolution
  • Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media
  • Internet access is helpful
  • Recommended: 3D Acceleration Capable Videocard with at least 256 MB
{#

#}
Virtualization
  • 2 GHz single core processor
  • 2 GiB RAM (system memory)
  • 25 GB of hard drive space
  • It’s also advisable to dedicate 128 MB of video memory to your virtual machine.
  • If possible, dedicating an extra CPU core, and an extra GB of RAM will greatly increase the responsiveness of your virtual machine
